Remote web developer: the advantages of working with this figure

The appearance of new professions in the digital environment, the improvement in connections and, above all, the change in mentality in companies have made it possible for remote work to grow exponentially in recent years. 

Have you ever worked with a remote web developer? At Yeeply we want to tell you about the advantages for your company of working with one .

Advantages of working with a remote web developer

Having a remote web development supposes a whole series of benefits for your company in many aspects. Finding a web programmer with specific knowledge may not be an easy task. 

Despite the fact that there are more and more professionals in the IT sector, the demand for these professionals is increasing, so there is more competition between companies to sign them.

This means that if you need to hire a web developer, there may not be many available in your city or willing to relocate . However, if you broaden your search for a web developer far and wide, the options grow enormously.

However, it is advisable to have professionals who work with a schedule similar to yours to avoid possible communication problems that we will see later.

On the other hand, the company saves costs by having one less worker in the office : electricity, space, computer and office supplies, etc., in addition to the employee's expenses. 

Productivity also often benefits from remote work. Keep in mind that many of the remote web developers tend to work from home . With this, daily trips to the office are avoided. 

Instead, people who work from home avoid that focus of tension. They already have a suitable space in which they feel comfortable to do their work, so they face the day in a different way .

Common remote work pitfalls and how to avoid them

Although the work of a remote web developer can be very beneficial, it can also bring some conflicts. There is nothing more effective than knowing the most frequent problems , in order to be able to anticipate them and solve them as soon as possible.

Lack of organization

Sometimes, having professionals who work from home, problems arise with the organization: problems appear to meet deadlines, answer emails ... With which you cannot be sure that the professional is dedicating himself to your project or not. 

To avoid this type of conflict, it is best to make the internal processes clear at the beginning of the project. If you choose an experienced remote developer , you probably won't have any problems in this regard. 

Lack of commitment to the project

Working remotely may mean that the remote developer does not have first-hand reference to the particularities of the company or project , nor does he feel integrated with the rest of the team. 

This can mean that you carry out your work without real involvement and aspects that could improve the final result are overlooked.

It is recommended that, even periodically or at the beginning of the project, the remote worker meets the rest of the team and (if possible) spends some time in the offices.

It is also equally important that each member of the team has a good understanding of the objectives of the project . It doesn't matter what your role or level of responsibility is. 

The simple fact of making him feel an active part and transferring the motivations of the project to him will make it easier for the remote programmer to become actively involved .

Communication problems

Communication problems are often the main cause of conflict in remote workers. By communicating remotely, the nuances of the conversation can be lost, personal problems that may affect professional development are ignored, etc.

It is important that team members have spaces in which to share information about themselves with others and communicate directly with the entire team . 

For example, the use of tools such as Slack , which allows both group communication with the team and private communication with each component, will help provide the team with this common space.

At the same time and occasionally, making video calls with platforms such as Skype or Hangouts can solve the problem of distances and minimize these misunderstandings in communication .

Tips for effective remote work management

To work with guarantees with a remote web developer, it is important to make the processes clear and provide tools that improve team communication. 

In addition, the use of agile methodologies will facilitate the day to day . Some of the tips to effectively manage remote work are the following:

  • Each member must be clear about their responsibilities. Putting the assignment in writing will help in case a member of the team has doubts about the person to whom she should go.
  • Frequently use programs such as Skype or Hangouts. Video calls help to empathize with the rest of the team members and devirtualize the remote programmer.
  • Simplifies day-to-day project management. Often, we tend to get lost in endless meetings that are not at all productive. Avoid unnecessary meetings and allow you to concentrate on your work.
  • Record in writing the progress of the project, news, agreements... The best way to do this is to use a project management software or tool in which the entire team is coordinated and the assignment of tasks, news , shared files, etc.
